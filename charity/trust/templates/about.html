ABOUT TRAVANCORE-TRUST

We aim to identify, allocate, empower and impart to every children and adult valuable. To set up a comprehensive centre for mental health and substance abuse, integrating psychiatry, clinical psychology and counseling.

To promote skill development Training programs, motivational class and camps, project preparation, agriculture, diary etc, Marketing support to the needy agriculture groups. Monitoring, auditing and continuous training.

To tackle and eliminate poverty by giving each individual, child and adult, the opportunity to learn, develop and contribute their skills to make a better world.

OUR AIM

  • To establish, develop, maintain and grant aid in cash or kind to hostels, hospitals, medical colleges, nursing institutions, dispensaries, maternities, child welfare centers and/or such other similar charitable institutions in India for the benefit and use of the general public including rehabilitation centres for nursing HIV infected people and their families providing basic necessities of food, medicine, clothing and shelter
  • Sanitation Mission (Waste Management Programme) - To educate the general public regarding solid waste management with a mission to tackle the growing concern of improper garbage disposal. Awareness about the solid waste project, which is a part of a clean world environment, workshops, supervision by the management team experts for setting up its unit on demand.
  • Education Support (Vyasa)- Through this service, the trust intends to provide continuing education for the children of economically disadvantaged families.

OUR MISSION

  • To provide basic necessities to people to help them live, by providing basic healthcare and expensive surgeries
  • To provide the best healthcare as humanly possible for the less fortunate living in this world
  • To take care of abandoned children and abused women and give them a second chance at living life.
  • To establish, maintain or grant aid to homes for the aged, orphanages or other establishments for the relief and help to the poor physically handicapped, disabled or mentally retarded the needy and the destitute, orphans, widows and aged persons and to provide them food, clothing, education or any other help.
